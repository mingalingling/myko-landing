How MyKo works

MyKo is a two-sided marketplace for wrestling training. Parents and wrestlers find and book lessons; coaches list their services, set their prices, and get paid safely through Stripe.

For parents & youth wrestlers

Discover qualified collegiate wrestlers and book private lessons in a few taps.

  • • Browse coaches by location, credentials, and weight class
  • • View pricing, availability, and practice location
  • • Book 1-on-1 or small-group lessons
  • • Pay securely with a saved card
  • • Receive confirmations and reminders before each session
Goal: better partners, better reps, better wrestling.

For college wrestlers (coaches)

Turn your experience on the mat into flexible income while helping the next generation of wrestlers.

  • • Create a coaching profile with bio, credentials, and hourly rate
  • • Set your availability and practice location
  • • Accept bookings from local wrestlers and families
  • • Get paid through Stripe Connect
  • • Track upcoming sessions and earnings in one place
You control your schedule, your rate, and your clients.

Payments, fees, and payouts

MyKo uses Stripe to process all payments and distribute payouts to coaches. We charge a small platform fee per booking and a $2 booking fee to the student.

Student payments

Parents and wrestlers pay securely using Stripe.

  • • Pay the coach’s lesson price
  • • A small $2 booking fee is added per lesson
  • • Card details are handled by Stripe, not MyKo

Coach earnings

Coaches receive 95% of their listed lesson price.

  • • If a coach charges $60, they receive $57
  • • MyKo’s 5% platform fee is deducted automatically
  • • Payouts are handled with Stripe Connect

MyKo fees

MyKo earns a small fee per lesson to support the platform.

  • • 5% of the coach’s lesson price
  • • $2 booking fee per lesson paid by the student
  • • All fee math is transparent to coaches and parents

Safety and NCAA-aligned usage

MyKo is designed around real coaching services and youth development, not pay-for-play. Coaches are paid for their time and instruction, not for team performance or school affiliation.

• MyKo facilitates private wrestling coaching sessions between coaches and families.
• Coaches are compensated for legitimate training services, not recruiting, performance, or game outcomes.
• Practice locations are visible before booking so families know where sessions take place.
• MyKo does not offer inducements, bonuses for wins, or compensation tied to where a student-athlete attends school.
