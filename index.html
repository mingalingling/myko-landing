Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyKo collects, uses, and shares information when you use the MyKo mobile app and related services.

Last updated: December 13, 2025 No in-app payments Marketplace (coaches + players/parents)

Information We Collect

We collect the information below to operate the app and enable core features like profiles, search, booking, and messaging.

  • Account: name, email, and user role (coach or player/parent), plus optional profile info (bio, credentials, sport, experience) and an optional profile photo.
  • Usage: messages between users, bookings and session details, and availability schedules.
  • Location & Addresses: if a player chooses “my facility,” the address is collected to share with the booked coach for that lesson.
  • Device & App Data: app version, device type, basic diagnostics, and (if enabled) a push notification token.
We do not collect government IDs or payment card numbers.

How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Match players/parents with coaches
  • Enable search, scheduling, bookings, and messaging
  • Provide reminders/notifications (if enabled)
  • Operate, secure, and improve the app
  • Offer support

Payments

MyKo does not process payments in the app. Any payments for coaching are handled directly between users outside the app (for example: cash, Venmo, or Apple Pay). MyKo does not store or process payment card information for coaching sessions.

Public vs. Private

  • Coach profiles (for example: name, sport, city/state, bio, and an optional Instagram link) may be visible to users in the app.
  • Player facility addresses are only shared with the booked coach for that lesson.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

  • We may share limited data with service providers that help run the app (for example: hosting, databases, image hosting, maps/locations, or analytics). These providers must protect your data and use it only to provide services to us.
  • We may share information if required by law, to protect rights, or to prevent fraud or abuse.

Device Permissions

  • Camera/Photos: to upload a profile image (optional).
  • Notifications: to send booking confirmations and reminders (optional; you can turn this off in system settings).

Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active and as needed to run the app. If you delete your account, we permanently delete your profile, messages, bookings, availability, and uploaded content, subject to lawful retention needs.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can update your profile in the app.
  • You can delete your account at any time: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  • For data access or export requests, contact us at nomomisoteam@gmail.com.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International Transfers

We may process and store data in the United States and other countries. These locations may have different data protection laws than your home country.

Children’s Privacy

MyKo is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a new “Last updated” date.
